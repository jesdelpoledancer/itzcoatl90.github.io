BUSINESS MODEL

  • The "EGI" business model is the result of applying conditional methodologies to participate in segments of proven market requirements, thus improving the efficiency of the business and providing added value.
  • The feasibility of the business will be the result of the participation of high profile specialists in their sector of expertise in order to guarantee the desired results.
  • To capitalize on the skills and facilities that (EGI) has, it seeks and develops consortium structures (companies such as SPV's) in which its operation guarantees the integrity and scope of each project.




OBJECTIVE MARKET

  • Any company that seeks to develop a project in the sectors of Infrastructure, Oil & Gas, agricultural, agroindustrial, road communication, water treatment, power generation and cogeneration, solid processing.
  • Support and financial structure. Support own projects and those contracted by third parties with an amount greater than 20.0 MUSD that are delayed for lack of resources.
    • Any short- and medium-term project between 20-50 MUSD
    • Any long-term project between 50-1000 MUSD
  • To be part and participate in companies that comply with the required terms of procurement, technique, administration and financial structure capable of developing large projects.




SECTOR

Once the project qualifies within the framework of the business model, EGI can participate in the operation and management of sectors such as:

  • FINANCIAL
  • ENERGY
  • OIL & GAS
  • INFRASTRUCTURE
  • AGRICULTURAL
  • HEALTH
