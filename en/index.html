HISTORY OF
ENERGLOBAL

Energlobal Investment Group Ltd., (EGI), is an investment fund established in Houston, Texas. This fund was created with the purpose of supporting and maximizing the investment opportunities generated by the market
The (EGI) Value added strategy is to act as a guarantor and / or financing vehicle for its own or associated companies dedicated to developing projects in the following sectors: energy, infrastructure , agricultural , agroindustrial, road communication, water treatment, power generation and cogeneration, solid waste processing and health services in Mexico.
Business structuring.- (EGI) seeks and develops consortium structures capitalizing on its skills and facilities which include, Engineering, Procurement and Construction, as well as international financing facilities directed to developers in these sectors.




MISSION

Integrate projects by providing operating strategies and structures resulting in an attractive feasibility and profitability with a controlled risk using financial structures and incorporating companies, technologies and top experts.




VISION

To be a multidisciplinary project integrating fund with global reach, boosting the growth of the company with investments and participation in national and international projects.
